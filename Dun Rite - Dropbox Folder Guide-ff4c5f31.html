Why It Works

1Number your folders

Prefix with 01_, 02_ so they sort in workflow order, not alphabetically.

2One project naming rule

Use YEAR-JOB#_Name. Everything sorts chronologically and stays searchable.

3Date files inside-out

Lead with YYYY-MM-DD so files auto-sort by date within a folder.

4Archive, don't delete

Move finished jobs into 02_Completed — keep Active clean and fast.
