DunRite needs one place to run the business — projects, schedule, budgets, commitments, billing, compliance, and closeout — without paying enterprise per-seat pricing for tools that don't fit. The DunRite OS is that system, tailored to your workflow and connected to the accounting and document tools you already use. This proposal covers hardening the platform for daily use, loading your real data, wiring your integrations, deploying to your domain, training your team, and keeping it running.

What's included

✓Portfolio & project management dashboard
✓Schedule, budget & schedule-of-values tracking
✓Commitments, directory, bidding & meetings
✓Inspections, daily logs & photo records
✓Invoicing & change-order routing with approvals
✓Subcontractor insurance compliance + auto-reminders
✓Owner Profit Advisor (AI) — money-focused guidance
✓Three-phase automatic job reconciliation at closeout
✓QuickBooks, Microsoft 365, Dropbox & Sheets links
✓Role-based access & email notifications
