Owner & Company Records

Executive File Organization Standard
Dun Rite Construction Group LLC
Rev. 2026.06

Owner-level files are company records, not job files — legal, financial, and HR documents that run the business. Keep them in their own top-level folder, restrict access, and group by function so tax time, audits, and renewals are a five-minute pull, not a scramble.

├─ 08_Vehicles & Equipment
├─ 09_Vendors & SubsW-9s, COIs
└─ 10_Owner Personalprivate — owner only

Inside 06_Taxes and 04_Financials, add a folder per year (2024, 2025, 2026) so records archive themselves.

Owner Rules

1Separate company from personal

Business records live in 03_Company. Anything personal goes in 10_Owner Personal — never mixed.

2Group by function, then year

Function first (06_Taxes), year subfolder second (2025). Renewals and audits pull instantly.

3Track expirations

Name renewable docs with their expiry: GL-Policy_exp-2026-12-31.pdf.

4Lock down access

Share 03_Company with the owner & bookkeeper only — never the project team.
